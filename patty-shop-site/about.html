About The Patty Shop

A Vancouver institution with Jamaican roots.

Since 1979, The Patty Shop has been serving generations of Vancouverites with handmade patties inspired by authentic Jamaican tradition.

What began as a small family-run operation grew into one of the city's most beloved quick stops for comfort food done right.
