A Vancouver Institution Since 1979

The Patty Shop started as a small family operation nearly five decades ago, bringing authentic Jamaican patties to Vancouver's neighbourhood.

For over 45 years, we've been crafting the same flaky, flavourful patties using recipes passed down through generations. Every patty is still made by hand.

We believe in keeping things simple: quality ingredients, authentic recipes, and genuine hospitality.
